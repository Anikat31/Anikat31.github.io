# Hello — I’m **Anikat Kankaria** *(अनिकेत कांकरिया)*

I am a researcher at the  
**International Centre for Theoretical Sciences – Tata Institute of Fundamental Research**  
([ICTS–TIFR](https://www.icts.res.in/), Bengaluru).

I currently work with  
[**Samriddhi Sankar Ray** *(সমৃদ্ধি শঙ্কর রায়)*](https://www.icts.res.in/people/samriddhi-sankar-ray).

<p>
My research focuses on <strong>far-from-equilibrium phenomena</strong>, with a particular
emphasis on <strong>turbulence</strong> and its underlying mathematical structure.
I am broadly interested in how ideas from <em>nonlinear dynamics</em>,
<em>stochastic processes</em>, and <em>statistical physics</em> emerge and interact
across complex physical systems.
</p>

---

## Research Interests

- Turbulence and non-equilibrium statistical physics  
- Dynamical systems and nonlinear phenomena  
- Stochastic modeling and random processes  
- Complex and interacting systems
