    <h1>Hello — I’m <strong>Anikat Kankaria</strong></h1>

    <p>
      I am a researcher at the
      <strong>International Centre for Theoretical Sciences – Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (ICTS–TIFR)</strong>,
      Bengaluru. I currently work with
      <a href="https://www.icts.res.in/people/samriddhi-sankar-ray">
        Samriddhi Sankar Ray
      </a>.
    </p>

    <p>
      My research focuses on <strong>far-from-equilibrium phenomena</strong>,
      with a particular emphasis on <strong>turbulence</strong> and its mathematical structure.
      I am broadly interested in how ideas from nonlinear dynamics and stochastic processes
      manifest across complex systems.
    </p>

    <h2>Research Interests</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Turbulence and non-equilibrium statistical physics</li>
      <li>Dynamical systems and nonlinear phenomena</li>
      <li>Turbulence in geophysics and atmospherics flows</li>
      <li>Stochastic modeling and random processes</li>
      <li>Complex networks and interacting systems</li>
    </ul>

    <p>
      I enjoy exploring conceptual and methodological connections that bridge physics
      with fields such as ecology and finance.
    </p>
