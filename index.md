I am a Research Scholar at the
[International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS–TIFR)](https://www.icts.res.in/){:target="\_blank" rel="external nofollow noopener"},
Bengaluru, India. I am currently working with
[Samriddhi Sankar Ray (সমৃদ্ধি শঙ্কর রায়)](https://www.icts.res.in/people/samriddhi-sankar-ray){:target="\_blank" rel="external nofollow noopener"}.

My research focuses on **far-from-equilibrium phenomena**, with particular emphasis on
**turbulence** and its mathematical structure. I am broadly interested in how ideas from
nonlinear dynamics and stochastic processes manifest in complex systems.

**Specific interests include:**
- Turbulence and non-equilibrium statistical physics
- Dynamical systems and nonlinear phenomena
- Stochastic modeling and random processes
- Complex and interacting systems
